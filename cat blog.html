Things Cats like

Cats are some of nature's most misunderstood creatures. Some believe cats are nothing more than adorable fur babies with mesmerizing eyes.

They are wrong, so wrong. Cats are adorable for a reason - they are trying to infiltrate your home and make you adopt them instead of having a baby of your own.

If you want to know more about cats: here's some facts for you.

  1. Cats like to be high above you looking down on you
  2. Cats like ignoring you when they are not hungry
  3. Cats like sticking their butt up in the air
  4. Cats like scratching your favourite furniture. The newer and more expensive that couch is, the better
Fact: Cats might eat you if you were small enough for them to clamp their jaws down on you. So beware, cats are worst than drop bears. That being said, I still prefer having one to having a housemate.

Why some cats are so zen


Some cats are just more chill than others. Reasons: They sleep at least 16 hours a day. When you cat nap more than half your life away, you are guaranteed to be full of energy.

Some cats are also huge fans of cat nip. Luckily for them, cat nip is totally legal unlike other plants humans are into.
